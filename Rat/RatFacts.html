Rat facts:

1. Smart little critters: Rats are super clever! They can learn tricks, like jumping through hoops or finding hidden treats. Just like kids, they love solving puzzles and figuring things out.

2. Best buddies: Rats are like tiny social butterflies! They make friends easily and love hanging out with each other. They have sleepovers, play games, and even give each other little rat hugs.

3. Food adventurers: Rats are foodies! They enjoy munching on all sorts of yummy stuff, like fruits, veggies, and grains. They're like tiny chefs always on the lookout for tasty treats.

4. Amazing climbers: Rats are like little acrobats! They can climb up walls and trees using their super-strong legs and claws. It's like having a tiny ninja in your backyard.

5. Family builders: Rats are expert moms and dads! They can have lots of babies called "pups" and take great care of them. It's like having a big, loving rat family all living together.

So, next time you see a rat, remember they're not just pests; they're actually pretty cool critters with lots of fun facts to discover!
